About Robes & Wizard Hats

Robes & Wizard Hats came to be due to a final project for a Mobile Application Development Class. This application was created to assist with designing characters for the roleplaying game Dungeons and Dragons. This is app is in no way affiliated with Dungeons and Dragons, Wizards of the Coast, Forgotten Realms, or Hasbro.
